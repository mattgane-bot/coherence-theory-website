Quick Coherence Assessment

Rate a system across all six coherence types for a rapid overview of where alignment holds and where it breaks. For deeper analysis, use the 19-Step Evaluation.

Aesthetic / Structural(inorganic)

How well does the physical form resolve forces, proportions, and spatial relationships?

Structure conflicts, proportions feel wrong, forms unresolvedBeautifully resolved geometry, stable structure, legible spatial order
Dynamic(inorganic)

How well does energy flow, circulate, and resolve through the system?

Energy stagnates, overwhelms, or dissipates chaoticallyEnergy flows smoothly, rhythms are balanced, movement is resolved
Connection to Nature(organic)

How well is the system integrated with natural systems, cycles, and landscapes?

Sealed from nature, no daylight, vegetation, or seasonal awarenessDeep ecological integration, natural materials, sensory connection to living world
Livingness(human)

Does the environment support human biological comfort, health, and the feeling of being alive?

Drains energy, causes discomfort, feels hostile to the bodyWarm, restful, naturally lit, human-scaled, sensory-rich
Societal(human)

Does the system support stable social interaction, cooperation, and collective life?

Social isolation, fragmented communities, no gathering spacesStrong social bonds, clear governance, shared spaces that support collective life
Symbolic(human)

Does the system carry cultural meaning, memory, identity, and symbolic charge?

Generic, anonymous, could be anywhere, no cultural memoryRich in meaning, memory, identity, and cultural continuity across generations
